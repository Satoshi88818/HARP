5. src/control/wbc.rs

Changes from v3:

Imp-4: micromath::F32Ext::invsqrt() replaces 1.0 / (1.0 + overheat).sqrt() in thermal de-rating. Maps to the vrsqrte.f32 ARM instruction (1–2 cycles vs ~28).

Imp-5: Semi-Implicit Euler integration for virtual spring displacement: velocity is updated first and the new velocity drives the displacement update, preventing resonant oscillation at high stiffness.

Imp-8: New Kani harness verify_thermal_attenuation proves the thermal solver is strictly monotonically attenuating (derating can never amplify a torque), and a second harness verifies output stays within MAX_HARDWARE_TORQUE_NM.
